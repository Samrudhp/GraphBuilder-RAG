Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist. He developed the theory of relativity and won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921. Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany in 1879.

Marie Curie was a Polish-French physicist and chemist. She conducted pioneering research on radioactivity and was the first woman to win a Nobel Prize. Curie was born in Warsaw, Poland in 1867.

Isaac Newton was an English mathematician and physicist. He formulated the laws of motion and universal gravitation. Newton published his work Principia Mathematica in 1687.
